Detailed Description

Mix mass-fraction beta SDE homogneous decay coefficients policy.

User-defined parameters b' and kappa' are constants in time and ensure decay in the evolution of <y^2>. Additionally, S is constrained to make d<rho>/dt = 0, where <rho> = rho_2/(1+rY).
