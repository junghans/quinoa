Detailed Description

template<class eq>
struct inciter::deck::check_eq< eq >

Do general error checking on the differential equation block.

This is error checking that generic equation types, such as advection-diffusion or the Poisson equation must satisfy. For more specific equations, such as Euler or compressible Navier-Stokes, a more specialized equation checker does and can do better error checking. See, e.g, check_compns.
